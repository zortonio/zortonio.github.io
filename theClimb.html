The Climb

In local mountaineering parlance in the western United States, a fourteener is a mountain peak that exceeds 14,000 feet in elevation. Over the span of two and a half months, Johnny will summit 53 of these peaks loacted in Colorado. Upon completion, Johnny will have traversed over 100,000 feet of elevation in his quest to climb for Honduras.
